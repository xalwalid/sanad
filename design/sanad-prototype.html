Your personal timeline

Week 1 — the steepest climb

Cravings, irritability and poor sleep peak around day three. Your body is recalibrating, not failing. Water, movement, busy hands.

Week 2 — the fog lifts

Sharpest symptoms ease. Mood steadies. Sleep and vivid dreams can stay unsettled a while — that's expected.

Week 4 — a new normal

Most people feel a real improvement. Now it's about replacing old routines with ones that serve you.

Week 8+ — protecting progress

The physical chapter is mostly behind you. Watch stress and boredom; lean on your routine.

A note for others

Quitting alcohol?

Heavy long-term drinkers can face dangerous withdrawal. Please talk to a doctor first — Sanad is a supportive tool, not medical care.
